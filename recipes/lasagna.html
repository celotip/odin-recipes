Lasagna

Description:

Quick and simple recipe to make your favorite lasagna!

Ingredients:
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
Steps (simplified):
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer Lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
